Building sustainable post-harvest systems free from pesticide dependency.

Designed for sustainable agricultural practices, CropWatch promotes smarter, eco-friendly post-harvest management. Through predictive analytics and practical recommendations, it helps reduce chemical reliance while preserving crop quality.

What we offer

1. Predictive Spoilage Analysis

  • Analyzes storage conditions like temperature, humidity, and storage duration.
  • Generates real-time predictions of spoilage or pest risk levels.

2. Prediction Data Upload

  • Users can upload storage information to the database.
  • The information uploaded will be used by the system to provide automatic daily updates of the storage conditions.

3. Smart Recommendations

  • Suggests sustainable, pest-free interventions based on predicted risks.
  • Enables farmers and users to act early and prevent loss.

4. History Dashboard

  • Displays recent and historical predictions of spoilage risk.
  • Helps evaluate how environmental factors influence storage conditions over time.
